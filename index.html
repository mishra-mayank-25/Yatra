Somnath(Gujarat)

The Somnath temple, also called Somanātha temple or Deo Patan, is a Hindu temple located in Prabhas Patan, Veraval in Gujarat, India. It is one of the most sacred pilgrimage sites for Hindus and is believed to be first among the twelve jyotirlinga shrines of Shiva..

Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva and is one of the twelve Jyotirlingams, shrines which are said to be the most sacred abodes of Lord Shiva.

Ganga Aarti, Varanasi

Ganga Aarti is an important ritual held on a grand scale every morning and evening on the banks of the holy Ganges. The priests perform the Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat. The entire Ghat gets illuminated with a divine light that can be felt strongly. The magnificent ritual involves huge brass lamps lit with oil and the priests chant the holy mantras that reverberate in the entire location.

21st may, 2022 by admin
Ratha Yatra, Puri

The Ratha Yatra of Puri, also rendered as the Ratha Jatra is a Hindu festival associated with the deity Jagannath held at Shri Kshetra Puri Dham in the state of Odisha, India.This Ratha Yatra is the celebration of the occasion of the deity Jagannath, a form of the Hindu deity Vishnu or Krishna, journeying towards his aunt's house.

09th october, 2022 by admin
Lalbaugcha Raja, Mumbai

The colourful and grand celebration of Mumbai’s Ganesh Chaturthi is usually associated with ‘Lalbaugcha Raja’ or ‘King of Lalbaug’, also popularly called Navasacha Ganpati or wish-fulfilling Ganesha.It is one of those grand and elebratore Hindu festivals which is celebrated over 10 days.
